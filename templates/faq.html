Everything you need to know about PhysiologicPRISM, the PRISM Framework, pricing, AI token usage, and how AI-powered clinical reasoning can transform your physiotherapy practice.

What is the PRISM Framework for physiotherapy?
PRISM is an 11-step structured clinical reasoning framework. It guides physiotherapists from patient presentation through pathophysiological mechanism analysis, ICF-based functioning assessment, differential diagnosis, SMART goal setting, and evidence-based treatment planning — with AI-generated suggestions at each step.

How is PhysiologicPRISM different from AI SOAP note tools?
Most AI physio tools transcribe or auto-fill SOAP notes. PhysiologicPRISM actively guides clinical reasoning — suggesting differential diagnoses, screening clinical flags, generating ICF-based functioning assessments, and producing evidence-referenced treatment plans. It's a clinical reasoning assistant, not just a documentation tool.

How are AI tokens used in Normal Mode vs. Quick Mode?
Normal Mode: AI suggestions are generated on-demand for individual fields when you click the "🧠 Suggest" button. Each field suggestion uses one AI call. This gives you maximum control but uses more AI calls per assessment (typically 15-25 calls for a complete assessment).

Quick Mode: Uses exactly 8 AI calls per complete assessment — one comprehensive AI call per screen (Patho Mechanism, Subjective, Initial Plan, Risk Flags, Objective Assessment, Provisional Diagnosis, SMART Goals, and Treatment Plan). All fields are pre-filled at once, making it 2-3x more AI-efficient than Normal Mode. Perfect for busy clinics or when you're running low on AI calls.
