## üìù CONTENT STYLE GUIDE (Based on Existing Blogs)

### Tone:
- Professional but approachable
- Educational, not sales-y
- Evidence-based with practical tips
- Structured with clear headings

### Structure:
1. **Introduction**: Why this matters (2-3 paragraphs)
2. **Core Content**: 3-5 main sections with subheadings
3. **PRISM Connection**: How the software helps (1 section)
4. **Conclusion**: Key takeaways (1-2 paragraphs)

### Length:
- **Short-form**: 800-1,000 words (SMM focus)
- **Medium-form**: 1,200-1,500 words (standard)
- **Long-form**: 2,000-3,000 words (comprehensive guides)

### SEO:
- Primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph
- 2-3 secondary keywords throughout
- Meta description 150-160 characters
- Internal links to related blogs
- External links to research/evidence
